Material Requisition with Stock on Hand Button

Product Stock on Hand Show on Material Requisition Odoo

This app allows users to show stock on hand button on material requisition form to Inventory user, Purchase user, Requisition User and Requisition Manager only.

Main Features:

  • Allow you to view Stock On Hand on a smart button on Material Requisition Form for display available quantity of products on requisition lines.
  • New Menu Items for Products, Product Variants and Stock On Hand have been added here on the requisition menu.
  • For more details please watch the video and below screenshots.

Menus Available:

  • Requisitions
    • Products
      • Products
      • Products Variants
      • Stock On Hand
